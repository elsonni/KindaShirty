Our Story

At KindaShirty, we’re not afraid to be a little bold, a little weird, and a whole lot of fun. What started as a side hustle making clever t-shirts for friends has grown into a full-blown mission to bring smiles, smirks, and side-eyes to wardrobes everywhere.

Whether you're celebrating the absurd, the nostalgic, or just love a good pun, we've got a shirt for that. All of our designs are printed in-house with attention to detail, quality, and sarcasm. Probably too much sarcasm.

Why Shop With Us?

  • Original designs that don’t take themselves too seriously
  • Printed with care on soft, high-quality materials
  • Locally produced — no mass drop shipping
  • Made by people who actually like t-shirts

Support Small, Stay Weird

We're a small independent business that believes in supporting creativity, individuality, and good vibes. Thank you for being here — we hope you find something that makes you laugh, nod in approval, or say "oh heck yes."
